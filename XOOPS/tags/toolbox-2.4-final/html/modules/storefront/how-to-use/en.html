How to Use (Reception)

With the Reception function, the Q&As stored in the Toolbox can be displayed on the computers at the reception counter in stores.

On this page, you will find a brief description of how to use the following functions:

  1. 1. Customization of Q&As for reception counter’s display
  2. 2. Reception window
1. Customization of Q&As for reception counter’s display
You can select Q&As to be shown at the reception counter’s display. You can link the answers to images and maps. Click "Customize" button in the line of the appropriate Q&As. Pairs of Q&As will be listed. Check the boxes of the pairs that you wish to show at the reception.
To link images and maps with the answers, click "Register" button in the line of the appropriate answer. To register an image, select one of the files stored in the File Sharing function. To register a map, enter the URL of the "link" of Google Maps. The URL is displayed when you click the "link." You can register up to two images or maps in total.
To show the meanings of the terms in the answers, click "Link a glossary" and select an appropriate glossary.
2. Reception Window
To start displaying a reception window, click a "Start" button in the line of the appropriate Q&As. First, specify the languages for a customer (=main language) and a receptionist (=sub language).
To show questions of a certain category, select the appropriate category on the window. To show answers, click the appropriate question. When images or maps are linked to the answer, they will be displayed on the right side of the window. When the answer includes terms in the linked glossary, the terms will be displayed in blue letters. To check the meanings of the terms, put the mouse over the terms.
